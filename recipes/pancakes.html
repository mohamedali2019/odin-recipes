Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast favorites made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter. Theyâ€™re golden on the outside and light on the inside, perfect for stacking high and drizzling with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix just until combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form on top and the edges look set, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.
