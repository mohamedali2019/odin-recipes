lemonade

Lemonade is a classic refreshing drink made by mixing freshly squeezed lemon juice with water and sugar. It’s the perfect balance of sweet and tart, ideal for cooling off on a warm day or serving at picnics and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4–6 lemons)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 5 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes (as desired)
  • Lemon slices and mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and the sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar completely dissolves to make a simple syrup. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice, cooled simple syrup, and the remaining 4 cups of cold water.
  3. Stir well and taste. Add more water or sugar depending on your preferred sweetness or tartness.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Serve over ice and garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves if desired.
