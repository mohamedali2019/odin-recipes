Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. The layers are baked until bubbly and golden, creating a comforting and hearty meal that’s perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup-grated Parmesan
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degree F (190 degreeC)
  2. Cook lasagana noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; sauté until softened. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, dried basil, and oregano. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 15–20 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
